About Us

At Pearls and Crumbs, We create beautifully designed, hand crafted bespoke cakes, cupcakes and cookies. Cake designer Ushba Adil runs the business from home offering highly personal and professional service.

Each cake is individually hand crafted with great attention to detail, so whether you want a sleek minimal design, pretty pastels or traditional wedding look. We can create a cake which tells your personal story.

All our cakes are baked using the finest ingredients to ensure they taste just as amazing as they look!
